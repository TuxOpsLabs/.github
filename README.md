## ✨ Who is TuxOps?

TuxOps is an independent technology studio focused on building modern software.

We enjoy creating platforms, tools, services, libraries and digital experiences that are thoughtfully engineered and built to last.

Our goal isn't to chase trends.

Our goal is to build things properly.

---

TuxOps serves as the home for a growing collection of projects, libraries and experiments.

## 🌍 Open Source

TuxOps follows a pragmatic open-source philosophy.
