# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering an open, welcoming and respectful environment for everyone.

Participation in TuxOps communities, repositories and discussions should be a positive experience regardless of:

* Experience level
* Education
* Nationality
* Personal background
* Identity
* Technical expertise

## Expected Behavior

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

* Being respectful and considerate.
* Providing constructive feedback.
* Accepting different viewpoints.
* Focusing on collaboration and learning.
* Acting professionally and respectfully.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Harassment or intimidation.
* Personal attacks.
* Discriminatory language.
* Deliberate disruption of discussions.
* Publishing private information without consent.
* Any behavior that creates an unsafe environment.

## Enforcement

Maintainers reserve the right to moderate discussions, remove content or restrict participation when necessary to maintain a healthy community environment.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all repositories, discussions, issues and community spaces managed by TuxOps.

## Contact

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers privately.
